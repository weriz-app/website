Vision

Our life's quality matters. Our own life style matters. Our time and our attention matters. Our awareness about surrounding matters. In weriz , we care about people's daily life, their needs and interests. Weriz lets you enjoy your favorite services provided locally, have fun and earn some small money meanwhile!

What We Do

By introducing local businesses and services (in a joyful way) to those local residents who have already shown interest, with the aim to meet their needs while making some profit for them, Weriz takes the world of Marketing to a win-win level and makes the local life very efficient! To do so, Weriz applied cutting-edge technologies and smart solutions by a professional team and collaborating partners.

Marketing Platform for Locals

Weriz is not revolutionizing advertisment world, instead, it simplifies the marketing process between consumers and local business owners in every part of the world and accounts as the global marketing platform for local businesses.

How Does It Work?

  1. Daily Needs and Interests

    In weriz, people tell about their daily needs and interests. Weriz then gives them only their relevant information and services provided by local businesses.

  2. Blockchain-based

    Blockchain enables all parties to interact in a transparent ecosystem allowing them to see what is really going on and assess their Weriz performance in a trustable platform.

  3. Customized

    Users can choose what info, how and when to receive. By doing so, everyone can benefit from his own customized Weriz!

  4. Monetize

    In Weriz, not only do the users have to pay for finding the local businesses they are looking for, they even get PAID for it.

  5. Location-Based

    Weriz works based on location which means it gives you only the information of local services nearby. Go anywhere around the world and enjoy your Weriz.

  6. Artificial Intelligence

    benefitting from Artificial Intelligence technology, Weriz sticks to a key marketing issue which is finding each user's potential needs and interests. He then will be informed of the related provider nearby.
